Welcome to Dawn Patrol, where the aroma of freshly brewed coffee mingles with the salty breeze, and every cup is a tribute to the spirit of the surf community.

At Dawn Patrol, we embrace the magic of early mornings, when the world stirs awake and the ocean beckons. Inspired by the unwavering dedication and camaraderie of surfers who greet the sunrise in pursuit of the perfect wave, we've fashioned a haven where coffee aficionados and adventurers unite to indulge their love for both the sea and exceptional coffee.

Conveniently nestled along the sandy shores, Dawn Patrol beckons with the promise of a seamless transition from surf to sip. Our beachfront location offers more than just stunning views; it's a practical haven for surfers seeking the perfect blend of adrenaline and relaxation. With surf racks thoughtfully stationed outside, you can effortlessly store your boards while you refuel with a steaming cup of coffee or a rejuvenating matcha latte. So whether you're catching waves at dawn or simply soaking in the coastal ambiance, Dawn Patrol is your go-to destination for a seamless blend of surf culture and coffee indulgence.

So whether you're gearing up for a sunrise surf session or just in need of a pick-me-up on your way to work, we invite you to join us at Dawn Patrol. Because no matter where your journey takes you, there's always time for one more wave and one more cup of coffee.
