Poplight: The Best No-Drill Wall Sconce You Didn’t Know You Needed

If you’ve ever wished you could upgrade your apartment’s sad lighting without getting yelled at by your landlord, you’re not alone. Most rentals come with two options: one harsh overhead bulb or... nothing. And while plug-in sconces exist, they’re usually either bulky, ugly, or require complicated installation.

That’s where Poplight comes in.

Poplight is a minimalist, wall-mounted light that looks like a hardwired sconce—but installs in minutes with no tools or damage. It plugs into any standard outlet, mounts with Command-style strips (or screws if you own your place), and gives off a soft, cozy glow that makes any room feel more intentional. It comes in multiple finishes—brushed brass, matte black, and limited-edition colors—so whether you’re going for vintage, modern, or something fun and unexpected, Poplight works with your style.

The idea behind Poplight came from wife-and-wife duo Rose Matthes and Caroline Matthes, who launched the product after moving into their first home together. Faced with the challenge of finding stylish, renter-friendly lighting that didn’t require drilling into their walls, they realized there simply weren’t any great options out there. So, they created their own. With backgrounds in business and design, the couple developed Poplight as a solution for renters, small-space dwellers, and anyone who wants beautiful lighting without permanent commitment.

No one wants to deal with complicated installations or risk losing their security deposit. Poplight makes it easy. Thanks to strong 3M adhesive tape, you can stick it directly onto your wall—no screws or tools necessary. It’s perfect for renters, dorm rooms, and even homeowners who just don’t want to commit to a permanent fixture. (That said, if you're someone who prefers screws, Poplight includes optional screw holes—no judgment here.)

But Poplight isn’t just about convenience—it’s also about style. Its minimalist design makes it a great fit for pretty much any room, whether it’s next to your bed, above your couch, or lighting up an entryway. It adds a cozy glow and elevates your space without demanding attention, acting as both a light source and a subtle statement piece.

Another thing that sets Poplight apart? It’s battery-powered and USB-C rechargeable. That means you can use it completely wire-free or keep it plugged in—whatever works best for your setup. No need to call in an electrician or deal with ugly cords snaking across your walls. The battery lasts a long time and charges fast, giving you both freedom and function.

Worried about removal? Don’t be. Poplight was designed to come off your walls just as easily as it goes up, leaving no residue or holes behind. When it’s time to move out—or just switch up your room—it peels off cleanly so your walls stay damage-free. No patching, no stress, just a clean slate.

Poplight also features adjustable brightness levels so you can control the mood. Whether you’re winding down for the night or need enough light to finish that late-night work session, Poplight makes it easy to set the right tone. It’s versatile lighting without the commitment.

So why choose Poplight over other no-drill wall lights? For starters, it doesn’t look like a no-drill light. It was designed to mimic the look and feel of high-end wired sconces—at a fraction of the cost and with none of the hassle. While most stick-on lights feel like a temporary fix, Poplight feels like a real design choice. It’s the one your friends will ask about when they walk in the room.

And let’s talk sustainability. Unlike battery-powered lights that eat through AAs like candy, Poplight’s rechargeable design means less waste and fewer trips to the store. It’s a smarter, greener option for lighting up your home.

But here’s the real game-changer: Poplight is the only stick-on wall light that’s intentionally designed to be removed. That means when it’s time to move apartments or just refresh your space, it comes off clean and goes right back up in your next place. It’s not just a product—it’s a lighting solution that moves with you.

If you’re tired of harsh overhead lighting, ugly lamps, or complicated installs, Poplight is the upgrade you’ve been waiting for. It’s renter-friendly, design-forward, and easy to love. Whether you’re decorating your first apartment or your fourth, Poplight helps your space feel a little more like you—without putting a single hole in the wall.
