Introduction

Couch Rodeo Magazine

Couch Rodeo Magazine will be an interior design magazine, specifically targeting renters and adults moving into their first place. I want Couch Rodeo to feature design techniques for smaller spaces, cater to adults with a lower budget, and be a space where people can find tips and tricks to curate their space
